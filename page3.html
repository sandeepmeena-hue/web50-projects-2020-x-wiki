This page cover my first year of college.After hardwork for two year i manage to get a seat in IIT delhi in mechanical branch.first time in my life i live away from my home in hostel.first few days are difficult for me as this is very new place for me.In hostel three students live in one room looking very congested but after some days we have no problem from any room and bathroom.I exactly remember my first day in college.I have workshop from 8am to 12am.In workshop we have to wear shoes which i don't know so i have to stand outside but TA called us as it was first day and they don't told us to wear shoes. then after 2 hours. break.I have only math and chemistry class. In august our college have fest which is ver nice.It run for four days and lots of students came from other college to see the fest.there were lots of cultural activity.It is biggest fest in north india.In college time goes fast and fast and our major exam came.i got 8.1 cgpa in first semester.After that we got 2 month break.we came in january for next semester and felt the real cold in delhi.It is very difficult to wake up early for the classes.Now something bad happens in mid of semester which is CORONA pandemic.So college closes due to this pandemic and I am waiting for college to reopen again.
